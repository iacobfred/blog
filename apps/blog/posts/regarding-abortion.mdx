# Regarding abortion

Abortion is a thorny issue. It has been a thorny issue for a long time, and it is especially so now,
following the Supreme Court's 2022 overturning of Roe v Wade and the actions of many states to
restrict access to abortion. Few topics can evoke such strong emotional responses from such a large
swath of the American people.

For many, abortion is a deeply personal issue. For me, at the time of this writing, it is not. But I
do have an opinion to share:

> Legislation regarding abortion must have a secular and rational basis independent of the religious
> beliefs of any of the American people.
